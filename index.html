Imran khan

Imran Khan (born 28 May 1984) is a Dutch-Pakistani[1] singer and songwriter. He became known in 2007 after his first single "Ni Nachleh" produced by Eren E.[2] Khan was signed to Prestige Records in late 2007, and released the single "Ni Nachleh" on the label.[2] His second single, "Amplifier" produced by Eren E was released on 13 July 2009, and later several others. His song "Satisfya" is his most viewed video till date for over 600 million+ views. His debut 15-track album Unforgettable was released on 27 July 2009 via Prestige Records,[3] produced by Eren E and Hakan Ozan.
